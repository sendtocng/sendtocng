What Is Hyperliquid Actually?

Hyperliquid is a decentralized perpetual exchange. Think of it as CME Group + Coinbase had a baby that lives entirely on-chain.

You can trade perpetuals (BTC, ETH, SOL, and 100+ other assets) with up to 50x leverage. Zero gas fees. Execution speeds that rival centralized exchanges.

That's the killer feature: CEX-level performance with DEX-level transparency.

Tokenomics: What's the $HYPE Deal?

Here's where it gets interesting:

Supply: 1 billion total $HYPE tokens. No inflation mechanism.

Distribution:

  • ~45% to community (airdrop + future incentives)
  • ~35% to team & contributors
  • ~20% to investors

Buyback Mechanism:

This is key. Hyperliquid uses protocol fees to buy back $HYPE from the market. Here's how it works:

  • Trading fees accumulate in the treasury
  • Treasury buys $HYPE from the open market
  • Bought tokens are distributed to stakers

The more people trade, the more fees, the more buyback, the more value flows to stakers. Classic flywheel.

Staking Yield: Current estimates put staking APY in the 8-15% range, depending on trading volume. Not shabby for a blue-chip DeFi asset.
